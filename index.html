FAQ

(Frequently Asked Questions)

What is a converter?

It is a tool that helps you quickly convert one value (for example, kilograms to pounds, meters to kilometers, calories to kilojoules, etc.) into another.

What is BMR and why should I calculate it?

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the number of calories your body burns at rest. Knowing your BMR helps you better understand your daily calorie needs.

What is the difference between BMR and AMR?

AMR (Active Metabolic Rate) is the calories you burn with physical activity (sports, walking, work). In short: BMR + activity = AMR

Can I use this converter every day?

Yes! It is designed for everyday needs: converting currencies, estimating calories, or checking measurement values.

How accurate are the calculations?

The formulas and conversion rates are standard and highly accurate, though minor differences may occur (for example, due to currency rate updates)
